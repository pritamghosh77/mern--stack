About Us

The Ideal Institute of Engineering is the premier destination for aspiring engineers seeking a transformative educational experience. Established in 2010 under the visionary leadership of Ideal Science and Research Welfare Hub, IIE has flourished as a paragon of excellence in engineering education. As a self-financed degree engineering & BBA college affiliated with MAKAUT and approved by AICTE, IIE is dedicated to cultivating industry-ready professionals under the guidance of experienced leadership. The Institute offers seven disciplines: CSE, CSE with AI/ML, ECE, EE, ME, CE, and BBA. Our curriculum emphasizes academic excellence, hands-on training, and innovation. Beyond the classroom, vibrant campus life and diverse opportunities empower students to grow personally and professionally.

Ideal Science and Research Welfare Hub

The Ideal Science and Research Welfare Hub is a registered trust dedicated to education, research, and social welfare, founded through careful planning and a shared commitment to philanthropy and academic excellence.

This noble initiative was founded by Shri Partha Sarathi Hesh and Dr. Raghupati Goswami, visionary leaders driven by purpose and a belief in education's power to inspire progress and knowledge.

Their mission was greatly supported by Mr. Bratish Kumar Sarkar, a respected entrepreneur known for his integrity, innovation, and social commitment. Motivated by the cause, he contributed his expertise and dedication to help bring the vision to life.

At Ideal Science and Research Welfare Hub, we believe education is key to societal progress. Our initiatives aim to empower individuals, promote intellectual growth, and uplift communities.
