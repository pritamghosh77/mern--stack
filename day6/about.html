About Us

The Ideal Institute of Engineering is the premier destination for aspiring engineers seeking a transformative educational experience. Established in 2010 under the visionary leadership of Ideal Science and Research Welfare Hub, IIE has flourished as a paragon of excellence in engineering education. As a self-financed degree engineering & BBA college affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT) and accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), IIE is dedicated to cultivating the future cadre of industry-ready professionals. Under the guidance of Partha Sarathi Hesh, honourable Secretary, Dr. Raghupati Goswami, dignified Chairman, and Mr. Bratish Kumar Sarkar, respected President, Ideal Institute of Engineering is steadily evolving into a premier institution. The Institute offers a diverse array of courses to cater to the aspirations of students. It offers seven disciplines namely Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), CSE with specializations in Machine Learning (ML) and Artificial Intelligence (AI), Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), Electrical Engineering (EE), Mechanical Engineering (ME), Civil Engineering (CE), Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA). At the heart of our institution lies a steadfast commitment to academic excellence, driven by a world-class faculty and state-of-the-art facilities. Our curriculum is meticulously designed to equip students with the knowledge, skills, and critical thinking abilities required to thrive in the ever-evolving world of engineering and Management.

Beyond the classroom, our vibrant campus life offers a plethora of opportunities for personal and professional growth. From cutting-edge teaching pedagogies to dynamic extracurricular activities, students at the Ideal Institute of Engineering are empowered to explore their passions and develop into well-rounded individuals.

Join us, and unlock a future filled with endless possibilities.
