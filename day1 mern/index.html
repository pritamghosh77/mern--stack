IDEAL INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING

The Ideal Institute of Engineering is the premier destination for aspiring engineers seeking a transformative educational experience. Established in 2010 under the visionary leadership of Ideal Science and Research Welfare Hub, IIE has flourished as a paragon of excellence in engineering education. As a self-financed degree engineering & BBA college affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology (MAKAUT) and accredited by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), IIE is dedicated to cultivating the future cadre of industry-ready professionals.
